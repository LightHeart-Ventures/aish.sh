# aish Positioning

## The Problem

Developers spend hours context-switching: terminals for commands, IDEs for code, dashboards for infra, chat for collaboration. Each context requires manual tool switching and loses the conversational thread.

## The Solution

**aish** is an **AI-native shell** that unifies developer workflows into a single conversational agent. Instead of typing `grep`, `git`, `curl`, you describe your intent in natural language. aish understands, delegates to specialized tools (via MCP), and streams results back — keeping you in flow.

## Target Audience

- **Developers**: AI-first workflows, less typing, more intent
- **DevOps/SRE**: Incident response, automation, real-time reasoning
- **Teams**: Shared agents, auditable workflows, collaborative shells
- **Researchers**: AI reasoning benchmarks, language model testing

## Positioning Statement

> **aish**: The AI-native shell. Replace `bash` with intent.
